Text equivalents for images

  • Provide alt text for all images
  • Make alt text succinct, avoid “image of”
  • Detailed images: text description inline or on another page
  • Image links: alt text describes function of image
  • Decoration: alt=""
  • For screen reader users and people with slow Internet
